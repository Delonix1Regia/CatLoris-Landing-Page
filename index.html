Catloris: Your Partner for a Healthier Tomorrow

CATLORIS FOR YOUR HEALTH

Catloris is an Android app that makes calorie tracking fun and engaging through gamification. With challenges, rewards, and progress tracking, it transforms nutrition management into an interactive e xperience. Designed to help you reach goals like weight loss or muscle gain, Catloris keeps you motivated to meet your daily targets for calories, protein, carbs, and fats.
